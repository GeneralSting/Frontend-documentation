HTML layout elements in more details:

	<main>
		-unique
		-one per page
		-shouldn't be nested
	<article>
		-block of related content that makes sense on its own without the rest of the page
	<section>
		-more grouping togheter a single part of the page
		-single piece of functionality
		-more sections in one article
	<aside>
		-content not directly related to the main content
		-provides additional information
	<header>
		-global header of parent element
	<nav>,<footer>
	-non-semantic wrappers:
		-div -> block
		-span -> inline
